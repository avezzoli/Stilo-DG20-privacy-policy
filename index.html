Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the app Stilo DG20, developed by Angelo Vezzoli for Stilo SRL.

Information Collection and Use

The app connects to an external device using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) in order to read and modify specific device parameters.

By default, the app does not collect or store any personal data.

Email Contact Form

The app includes an optional contact form where users may voluntarily provide their email address to request assistance or receive replies from Stilo SRL.

This email address is used solely for communication purposes and is not shared with any third parties.

Permissions Required

  • Bluetooth: Required to establish communication with the external BLE device.
  • Location access: May be requested on certain Android versions to enable BLE scanning. No location data is collected or stored by the app.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app communicates directly with the connected device and does not send data over the internet, except when the user explicitly submits their email address through the contact form.

Data Security

The app does not store data permanently or use external servers. Email submissions are sent securely to Stilo SRL and handled in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

User Rights

Users who provide their email address may request its deletion by contacting us at the address below. No other personal data is collected or retained.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
